Wall-E Comes Alive and Terrorizes the City

Earlier this week, Wall-E was seen wandering the streets of Grand Rapids in the search of an unknown person from whom he stole a small stick. He tore through the streets at break-neck speeds upwards of 0.5 mph. Bystanders were tormented by his antics, and many are still recovering. Fear and desperation were the theme of this tragic event.
